About SKYFE

Who We Are

Skyfe is a fortnite clan that was created in 2025 and is a group of players who are good at the game and know what they are doing. We will often host tournaments and events for everyone in our clan (and sometimes outside of the clan) to have fun and play. We do require players who are trying out to do a Google Form and to do tryouts with our team members.
